Two applications, various ways to use it

Our two applications let you operate, manage and use VTOL aircraft and vertiports for a variety of vertiports.

Cargo and freight air transportation

Our network provides smooth software to automate flight-plans for in-house freight or public exploitation.

  • Let users schedule cargo flights, both small and large orders
  • Decide on repurpose of deadhead flights for your private flights
  • Use our extensive schedular to make entire AI Generated flight plans
  • Automate your regular cargo shipment with our enterprise subscription

Passenger travel

Midrange passenger travel of the future will be VTOL. Our network provides A/B travel exploitation and is prepared to have on-demand flight.

  • One end-user app thats included in all our subscription models
  • Restrict vehicles and vertiports to only be used by certain users
  • Manage your vertiports and vehicles and dispatch them on the network
  • Automate flight sales and get paid per month

Manage your assets

We have an extensive management system where you can manage any of your aircraft, vertiports and user (restrictions).

  • Let our system do calculate the most efficient and safe flight plans
  • Setup user roles and restrictions
  • Manage your vertiports, including use rights
  • Manage your Vtol fleet and set rules on how they can be used
